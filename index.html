Memory Game

How to Play??

  • Enter the number of cards to be included in the game and click the submit button.
  • Click on the "Start Game" button to start the game.
  • All the cards will reveal thier images for a short period.
  • As, the cards gets covered, match the identical cards, in the pair of 2, to increase your score.
  • If you matched non-identical cards, Failed Attempts will increase.
  • On every successful match, your score increase by 2.
  • On every un-successful match, Failed Attempt increases by 1.
